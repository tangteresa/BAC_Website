Get involved!

We have fun events all the time so you can interact with the cats and learn more about the adoption process! Keep up to date with our schedule below.

Daily Events

Event Name Time Description
Coffee Cat Chats 8 AM to 9 AM Ask us your questions about anything from adoption to cat care over a hot cup of coffee, or just come to study or hangout with the cats! We will have warm beverages in the lobby.
Walk-in Meet and Greet 8 AM to 5 PM Drop by and get to know the kitties! The cats love attention, so even if you aren't sure about adopting, come by anyway to play and hang out with these furballs!
Volunteer Hours 3 PM to 5 PM No sign-up required! Just pop in and get ready to get messy. We always appreciate help with cleaning the cat enclosures and brushing or feeding the cats. Bath time is on Saturdays! We recommend wearing close-toed shoes and old clothes that can be clawed.
